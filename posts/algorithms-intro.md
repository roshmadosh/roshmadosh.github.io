Algorithms are the foundation of computer science.
Whether you're sorting a list or finding the shortest path, algorithms are at work.

### Big O Notation
It's essential to understand the efficiency of your algorithms!
1. O(1) - Constant time
2. O(n) - Linear time
3. O(n log n) - Log-linear time
